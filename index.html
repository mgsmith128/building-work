Possible causes of damp

1/ Pointing missing from jambs (next doors responsibility)
2/ The flashing over the ridge needs the sealing at the sides (your responsibility)
3/ Next doors coping stones are in a poor condition, the first step is to repoint the joints, but more major work could need carrying out to see what is actually under the copings?
4/ More gaps in the copings (next door)
5/ This is the rear which is out of sight. It is clear the bottom of the gable has not been repointed (next door)
6/ The coping stone is in poor condition (next door)

My general view is the repointing of the gable end is to a very poor standard. The images show areas which haven’t been repointed. The coping stone at least need repoint, I’m not confident about how things will look underneath the copings. The seal on your flashings needs checking.
